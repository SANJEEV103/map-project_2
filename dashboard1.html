191 people in the age group 18-25 do not have sufficient facilities to pursue their careers, nor do they have enough educational institutions available. However, 126 individuals have access to educational facilities.

For the age group 26-40, 96% are unemployed, and yet, 67% state that they have basic amenities available.

Among those aged 41-60, a majority are not receiving the benefits of government schemes, though they do receive free rations regularly.
